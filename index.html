Rent Choice


Find out which product you may be eligible for.


Q1: Are you an Australian citizen or permanent resident?



Private Rental Assistance, including Rent Choice is only available to Australian citizens including permanent residents residing in New South Wales (NSW).

Q2: Are you currently a social housing tenant?

What is social housing?


You may be eligible for:

Rent Choice Transition



You may also be eligible for:

  • Statement of Satisfactory Tenancy. For current or former public housing tenants. It can be a reference for rental applications.
  • Private rental brokerage service. Help for people with complex needs (and their support agencies) to set up a private rental tenancy.
  • Tenancy guarantee. Gives extra cover for rental arrears or property damage. It is an incentive to offer a lease to people.
  • Rentstart Bond Loan. An interest-free loan to help eligible people pay a rental bond
  • Advance Rent for Bond Loan. Clients and people moving into low-cost accommodation. It helps with deposit payments.
  • Tenancy Assistance. Help with rent and water arrears for eligible private tenants.


For information about these products call the Housing Contact Centre on 1800 422 322.
