Private Rental Assistance


Find out which product you may be eligible for.


Q1: Are you an Australian citizen or permanent resident?



Private Rental Assistance, including Rent Choice is only available to Australian citizens including permanent residents residing in New South Wales (NSW).

Q2: Are you currently a social housing tenant?

What is social housing?


You may be eligible for:

  • Rentstart Bond Loan. An interest-free loan to cover part or all of the rental bond for eligible people moving into a private rental.
  • Advance Rent for Bond Loan. A grant to cover the security deposit or advance rent for eligible people moving into caravan parks, registered boarding houses and hostels.
  • Tenancy Assistance. A grant of up to four weeks’ rent to cover unpaid rent and/or water charges. It may be offered once in a 12-month period.
  • Tenancy Guarantee. Up to $1,500 paid to agents and landlords to cover unpaid rent and/or property damage over and above the rental bond. It offers an incentive for agents and landlords to accept tenants.
  • Private rental brokerage service. One to one help for eligible people with complex needs to find and keep a private rental.
  • Statement of Satisfactory Tenancy. A reference from FACS Housing that tells a future agent or landlord that you were a satisfactory tenant when you lived in public housing.


For more information on eligibility requirements visit your local Housing Office or call our Housing Contact Centre on 1800 422 322
